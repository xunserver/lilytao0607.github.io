Master-Disciple Relationship

Disciple of Master He Shaojun

In 2011, Lily Tao was officially accepted as a disciple by Master He Shaojun, receiving systematic, complete, and rigorous training in traditional Yang Style Tai Chi.

Following her master's spirit, she treats Tai Chi as a path of spiritual cultivation and sees dissemination as her mission. She now teaches traditional Yang Style Tai Chi in Netherlands and is beloved by European students.

As a bearer of Yang Style Tai Chi, Instructor Tao is dedicated to passing on the most traditional and authentic Tai Chi culture to every student.

Master's Words

"Practicing Tai Chi forms is only the exterior. Real skill lies in basic skills. Without basic skills, all forms, push hands, and applications are like a house without foundation."

In decades of teaching experience worldwide, Master He Shaojun always emphasizes the importance of basic skills. Instructor Tao inherits this philosophy in Netherlands and strictly implements the master's order, considering "basic skill training" as the most important, most fundamental, and indispensable core content of the courses.
